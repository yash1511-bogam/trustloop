# Plans, Billing, and Alerts

This guide covers plan quotas, payment recovery behavior, reminder automation, and escalation policies.

## Plan Quotas

TrustLoop enforces daily usage limits based on your workspace plan tier.

### Daily Allowances by Plan

| Plan | Incidents | AI Triage Runs | Customer Updates | Reminder Emails |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Starter | 50 | 100 | 100 | 120 |
| Pro | 200 | 300 | 300 | 500 |
| Enterprise | 1,000,000 | 1,000,000 | 1,000,000 | 1,000,000 |

Review your plan allowances before high-volume incident periods to avoid hitting limits during active operations.

## Payment Lifecycle

TrustLoop does not immediately suspend workspace access when a payment fails.

### Payment Failure Sequence

1. Billing status transitions to past due.
2. Workspace owners and managers receive payment failure notification emails.
3. If payment is not resolved within 48 hours, the workspace is automatically downgraded to the Starter plan.

Treat payment failure notifications as operational issues requiring prompt attention.

## Reminder Automation

TrustLoop automatically sends reminders while incidents remain open.

### Standard Reminder Behavior

- Open incidents trigger reminder emails to the assigned owner.
- Reminder frequency is determined by workspace quota settings and incident severity.
- Reminders stop automatically when the incident is resolved.

### P1 Escalation

For P1 incidents, TrustLoop escalates beyond email using the following channels:

- Assigned incident owner
- Workspace managers and owners
- On-call rotation (when enabled)
- Browser push notifications
- SMS (when phone numbers are configured)

## Troubleshooting

### Reminder emails are not arriving

Verify the following:

- The incident has an assigned owner
- The incident status is not resolved
- The workspace has not exhausted its daily reminder email allowance

### P1 SMS alerts are not arriving

Verify the following:

- The owner or on-call responders have phone numbers saved in their profiles
- The workspace is configured for urgent escalation

### Push notifications are not arriving

Verify the following:

- Push notifications are enabled in the browser
- Notification permission has been granted
- The browser session has an active push subscription

## Billing Management

Navigate to **Settings → Billing** to:

- Upgrade or change plan tiers
- Apply coupon codes
- Review current billing status
- Check for payment failure notifications

<Callout type="info" title="Review escalation coverage proactively">
  The best time to verify reminder behavior, plan allowances, and escalation coverage is before a production incident. An internal drill surfaces configuration gaps while the operational impact is low.
</Callout>
